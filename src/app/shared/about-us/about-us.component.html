About Us

Welcome to the Global Donation Initiative - a compassionate organization dedicated to making positive changes in the world. Founded in 2023 by Dimitar Klunkoff, our mission is to facilitate and inspire meaningful giving, connecting generous donors with impactful causes and projects.

At Global Donation Initiative, we believe in the power of collective efforts to drive change. Our platform provides a seamless and secure environment where individuals and organizations can come together to make a difference. By harnessing the potential of modern technology, we strive to create a global community committed to philanthropy and social responsibility.

As we continue to grow and expand our reach, we remain committed to transparency, integrity, and accountability. Your trust is of paramount importance to us.

Thank you for joining us on this journey of making the world a better place. Together, we can create a lasting impact and inspire positive change.
